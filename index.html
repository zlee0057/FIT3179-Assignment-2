Migration Flow Around the World

Migrant Flow via Income Group

With the exception of high-income countries, the individuals from other income groups want to leave their countries. Middle-income groups seek improved economic prospects and living conditions, while low-income migrants often move out of necessity to escape poverty and adverse circumstances. In 2020, however, migration rate took a nosedive due to the spread of Covid-19.

Migrant Flow via Development Group

Developmental group, which refers to the level of development in a region or country, significantly impacts migration flow. Individuals from less developed regions may migrate to more developed areas in search of better opportunities, education, and living conditions. This was near impossible to do, however, in 2020 due to the spread of Covid-19.

This visualisation is created by Lee Zhi Yong. The dataset is taken from United Nations: Population Division.
